My Neighbor Totoro

となりのトトロ • 1988

My Neighbor Totoro follows the story of Satsuki Kusakabe and her younger sister, Mei. They (along with their father) move into the rural side of Japan so they can live closer to their mother who is hospitalized. There, they meet magical spirits that reside within the forest. Follow their little adventures with their fluffy new friend, Totoro.

Where to watch?

Netflix

Thoughts?

My Neighbor Totoro is a very fun watch. You'll realize instantly that you'll be in for a relaxing two hours, and I enjoyed every bit of it. There was no villain or huge conflict, just pure comfort and wondrous magic.

I'd definitely watch it again if I need a movie to wind down.


4.5 Stars
