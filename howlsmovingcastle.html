Howl's Moving Castle

ハウルの動く城 • 2004

Howl's Moving Castle follows the adventures of Sophie who, after a spell was cast on her, had her life flipped upside down. She meets a powerful wizard and finds a family in the tenants of the Moving Castle.

Thoughts?

Howl's Moving Castle is a beautifully crafted film with wonderful bits of art everywhere. I almost can't believe this came out in 2004! I loved every second of it, and how it's oozing with bits of personality everywhere.

The film is wonderful. It shows us a world where everything makes almost no sense, yet I somehow understand adore and every bit of it.


5 Stars
